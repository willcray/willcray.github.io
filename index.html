Hi! I'm Mike Verdicchio, a software engineer at Capital One working on Wikibuy and Paribus in New York City. I grew up a few miles north of Richmond, VA in Mechanicsville. I graduated in 2017 from the University of Virginia, earning my Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering in the School of Engineering & Applied Science.

I'm definitely a collector of interests. I'm fascinated by electronics and low-level firmware/driver development, which led me to studying computer engineering instead of computer science. I'm passionate about entrepreneurship, empowering aspiring engineers, and a long life of education. I'm also a firm believer in the UVA mentality of a Jeffersonian Engineer: one that can do great engineering work, but also understand the societal impacts of their designs.

In the far future, I see myself as a technology pioneer. Being an engineer, my end goal is to design things that excite people in this world. I'm interested in being a part of developing the circuitry and controls behind cutting-edge electronics that bring new and improved ways of living our lives, such as artificially intelligent robots, engine controllers, and audio electronics. For this reason, I see a graduate degree in my future, either in engineering or business. I'm also very interested in Patent and Intellectual Property Law and hope to one day incorporate that into my career.
